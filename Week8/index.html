Data Visualization

While this project is not quite complete yet, based on the course Syllabus, I am interested in working with the "Chaos Walking" book series, by Patrick Ness. This was one of my favourite series in my teens, as the construction of language and use of perspective were fascinating. I also simply love a good dystopian story. I would like to use data visualizations to compare the changing perspectives throughout the series. For example, the main character views an alien race as hostiles, and by the end of the series, comes to learn that the enemies are actually the townspeople he aligns himself with.
